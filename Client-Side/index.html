Few Words About Us

Minute Maids is a housekeeping company that provides quality cleaning services that is professional, caring, and warm. We provide General,Moving-in, Moving-out, Bathroom,Bedroom, Living Room,Kitchen, Garage, and Office Cleaning. We also have extra services like Fridge, Oven, and Window Cleaning. We also provide Laundry and Closet Organization Services to our clients.

Services

We offer cleaning services according to your needs

General Cleaning


Bedrooms, bathrooms, kitchen and living room are wiped and washed using cleaning products such as detergent, water, disinfectant and more.

Moving-in Cleaning

Performing a move in clean before the new homeowners and tenants unload their personal belongings. New living space has been scrubbed and disinfected from top to bottom and every inch of the room that needs attention.

Moving-out Cleaning

Proper cleaning is done after you leave you place to give respect to the next. We make sure that the floor is well cleaned and mopped, windows and ceiling are dustless and the whole room as presentable as possible.

Bathroom Cleaning


We provide comfort by properly scrubbing and disinfecting the whole surface, cleaning the toilet and sink and properly disposing and replacing garbage bags.

Bedroom Cleaning


Neatly organize your bedroom. All around room cleaning and dusting. From the Floor , windows up to the ceiling.

Living Room Cleaning


Safely clean and organize your belongings with care. We also sweep, mop and polish the floor for better ambiance.

Kitchen Cleaning

Providing a variety of services to your kitchen such as organizing your kitchen stuff and much more. This services does not include extraction (hood) cleaning, filter rental and cleaning, servicing deep fryers (change/filter), full kitchen cleans, cleaning of ovens, buying and selling of second hand equipment and more.

Garage Cleaning


Organize and clean all the mess in you garage. This service makes your garage a cleaner and clearer place.

Office Cleaning<

All kinds of room(Executive rooms , conference room, Individual rooms etc.) It will be well cleaned in order to achieve its good smell and better looking view. Floors, ceiling, walls will be cleaned. Workspace are thoroughly cleaned and disinfectants will also be applied

Extra Services


Extra services includes Fridge, Oven, and Window cleaning. Closet organization and Laundry is also offered.
